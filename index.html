Hi, I'm James Jeffryes, a PhD candidate at Northwestern University and the developer of the MINE databases.

I am passionate about using in silico modeling of cellular metabolism to understand complex biological systems and speed the development of therapies which improve quality of life. I'm always open to opportunities for collaboration or post-doctoral study and I look forward to hearing about your research.
