Hi, I'm James Jeffryes, a PhD candidate in at Northwestern University and the developer of the MINE databases.

I am passionate about using in silico modeling of cellular metabolism to understand complex biological systems and speed the development of drugs which have the potential to improve quality of life both in the developed world and in resource poor settings.
